Mindful Moments

A website dedicated to guiding users through mindful breathing, reflection, and listing exercises to help find calm and clarity in everyday life.

Site Name

Mindful Moments - Chosen because it evokes the concept of deliberately spending small, meaningful periods of time practicing mindfulness and self-awareness.

Suggested domain availability: mindfulmoments.com

Site Purpose

This site provides users with easy-to-follow mindfulness activities, including guided breathing, reflection prompts, and listing exercises that promote mental well-being. The goal is to help users incorporate mindfulness into their daily routine through simple and engaging practices.

Scenarios

  • How do I start practicing mindful breathing when I feel stressed?
  • What reflection prompts can help me recognize my personal strengths?

Color Schema

Primary Blue: Used for headings, links, buttons, and highlights
Light Gray Background: Used for page background and card sections

Typography

  • Outfit (Google Font) - Used for all headings to create a modern, elegant tone.
  • Inter - Used for body text and paragraphs for excellent readability.
